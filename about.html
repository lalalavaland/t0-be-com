About t0-be

A curated resource for builders, creators, and entrepreneurs.

t0-be.com was created with a simple belief: everyone has something they want to build, create, or become. The gap between wanting to build something and actually building it is not talent or resources. It is clarity, direction, and the right information at the right time.

That is what we are here to provide.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and equip builders with the practical guides, strategies, tools, and ideas they need to turn their ambitions into reality. Whether you are launching your first side project, building a micro-SaaS product, exploring passive income streams, or automating your online business, t0-be is designed to give you clear, actionable information without the fluff.

We believe in substance over hype. Every article we publish is written with one goal: to help you take the next step. Not someday. Today.

What We Cover

Side Projects

Practical frameworks for turning weekend builds into real products that generate income. Validation, monetization, and launch strategies that work.

Micro-SaaS

Step-by-step guides for building small, focused software products as a solo founder. From idea to paying customers, without the startup overhead.

Automation

Honest reviews and guides on AI tools and automation platforms that actually save time and money for online businesses.

Digital Products

How to create and sell templates, courses, tools, and other digital products. Build once, sell forever.

Open Source

Contributing to open source, building in public, and growing your reputation as a developer and creator.

Freelance & Services

Productizing your skills into repeatable, scalable service offerings. Fixed scope, fixed price, sustainable growth.

Who We Are

t0-be is built by builders, for builders. We are a small team of developers, creators, and entrepreneurs who have been in the trenches of building online businesses. We have launched side projects that went nowhere, built tools that found their audience, and learned the hard way what works and what does not.

Every piece of content on this site comes from real experience. We do not write about things we have not done ourselves. If we recommend a strategy, it is because we have tried it. If we suggest a tool, it is because we have used it.

Our Values

  • Practical over theoretical. We prioritize actionable advice that you can use today, not abstract frameworks that sound smart but do not help you ship.
  • Honest over promotional. We tell you what works and what does not, including the downsides and trade-offs. No affiliate-driven hype.
  • Builders first. Everything we create is designed to help people who are actively building. If you are here to build, you are our people.
  • Respect for your time. We write concise, well-structured content because your time is valuable and you have things to build.
